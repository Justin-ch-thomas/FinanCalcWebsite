Cost of Missing Discount

This equation is used to determine the cost of not taking advantage of a discount offered for early payment. $D$ is the discount rate, $F$ is the number of days to the final payment date, and $L$ is the number of days to the last discount date.

Equation

\(Cost \ of \ Missing \ Discount = \frac{D}{100 − D}×\frac{365}{F − L}\)
