Operating Profit Margin

Operating profit margin is a measurement of what proportion of a company's revenue is left over after paying for variable costs of production such as wages, raw materials, etc.

Equation 1

\(Operating Profit = {Net Revenue - Cost of Goods Sold - Labor - General Admin Expenses} \)

Equation 2

\(Operating Profit Margin = {Operating Profit / Net Revenue } \)
