Weighted Average Cost of Capital

This equation calculates the Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC) using the following elements:

r(E) The cost of equity.
w(E) The weight of equity in the company's total capital structure.
r(D) × (1 − t) The after tax cost of debt.
w(D) The weight of debt in the company's total capital structure.

Equation

\(WACC = r(E) × w(E) + r(D) × (1 − t) × w(D)\)
