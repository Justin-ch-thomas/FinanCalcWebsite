DuPont Method

The DuPont Method, or DuPont Identity is used to calculate the Return on Equity (ROE).

Equation

\(ROE = profit \ margin × asset \ turnover × equity \ multiplier\)

or

\(ROE = \frac{net \ income}{sales} × \frac{revenue}{total \ assets} × \frac{total \ assets}{shareholder \ equity}\)
