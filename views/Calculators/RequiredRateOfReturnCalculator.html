Required Rate Of Return

This equation is used to determine the Required Rate Of Return of a common stock

Equation

\(RR = G+{\frac{D}{P}} \)

RR = Required Rate Of Return

P = Price of the stock today

D = Dividend at the end of the year

G = Constant growth rate in dividends
